FAQs

How does SkillWell support placements?

Learners follow a guided path: core track completion, projects, career sprints, mock interviews, and referrals where available. Support continues through shortlisting and offer discussions.

Are the courses suitable for freshers?

Yes. Tracks are designed for college students and early‑career professionals, with foundations plus applied work so HR teams can see progression over time.

Can colleges or HR teams partner with SkillWell?

Yes. Institutions and organisations can onboard batches, access progress dashboards, and request custom reports for assessments and hiring pipelines.
